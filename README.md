### About me

I build end-to-end AI vision systems — from the first conversation with a user to a deployed product running reliably in the field. My approach covers the full development cycle:

**Problem identification & planning:** Working directly with end users (engineers, clinicians) to understand the real problem, define measurable success criteria, and design a realistic solution scope.

**Hardware & data acquisition:** Setting up and integrating industrial cameras and vision hardware, configuring lighting, triggering, and capture pipelines to collect high-quality image data under real operating conditions.

**Data processing & annotation:** Building custom web-based annotation tools for labelling image datasets (polygon segmentation, bounding boxes), managing dataset versions, and ensuring label quality.

**Model training & optimization:** Selecting, training, and fine-tuning deep learning models suited to the task — balancing accuracy, inference speed, and hardware constraints — then optimizing and validating them for reliable production deployment.

**Deployment & integration:** Delivering solutions as GPU-accelerated desktop applications or web-based services, integrated with hardware I/O, session logging, and user-facing controls.

**Feedback & iteration:** Collecting real-world feedback from operators and clinicians, diagnosing failure modes on live data, retraining with corrected or expanded datasets, and releasing updated builds.

The goal at every step is a product that non-technical users can trust and operate independently.
